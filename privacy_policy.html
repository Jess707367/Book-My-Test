Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Info: Name, email, phone number, and test preferences.
  • Payment Data: Processed through secure gateways (eSewa, Khalti, connectIPS, or cards).
  • Device & Usage Data: Non-identifiable info for performance and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account or bookings.
  • To send confirmations, reminders, and updates.
  • To improve user experience and app performance.
  • To maintain secure payments and transactions.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and security measures to protect your information. Your payment data is processed securely through trusted third-party providers, and we never store sensitive card details.

Sharing of Information

We may share limited data with trusted partners only when necessary:

  • Payment gateways for transaction processing.
  • Technical partners that help operate our app or website.
  • Authorities, if required by law or regulation.

Your Rights

As a user, you can:

  • Access or update your personal data anytime.
  • Request deletion of your account or information.
  • Withdraw consent to receive promotional messages.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain site functionalities.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal obligations. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated date.
