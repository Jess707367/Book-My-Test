Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about booking your test, rescheduling, or payments? We’ve got all your answers here.

Answers to YOURS Questions

Booking

How do I book a test through Book My Test?
Simply visit our website, select your preferred test, choose a center and date, fill in your details, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details.
Can I choose my preferred center and date?
Yes! You can browse available test centers and dates during the booking process and select the one that works best for you.
What documents are required during booking?
You'll need a valid government-issued ID (passport, citizenship card, or driving license) and your passport-sized photograph. Make sure your ID matches the name you use during booking.

Rescheduling

How can I change my test date?
Log into your account, go to "My Bookings", select the test you want to reschedule, and choose a new date from the available slots. Rescheduling fees may apply.
Are there rescheduling fees?
Yes, rescheduling fees vary depending on how close to the test date you make the change. Early rescheduling (7+ days before) typically has lower fees.
How far in advance can I reschedule?
You can reschedule up to 48 hours before your test date. Last-minute changes may not be possible depending on the test provider's policy.

Refunds

Am I eligible for a refund if I cancel?
Refund eligibility depends on when you cancel. Cancellations made 7+ days before the test may receive a partial refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are typically non-refundable.
How long does the refund take to process?
Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days. The time for the funds to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider.
Where can I check my refund status?
You can check your refund status in the "My Bookings" section of your account or contact our support team for an update.

Results

When will my test results be available?
Result timelines vary by test type. Most results are available within 5-13 business days. You'll receive an email notification when your results are ready.
How can I download or print my score report?
Log into your account, go to "My Results", and click the download button next to your test. You can save it as a PDF and print it directly.
Who can I contact if my results are delayed?
If your results are delayed beyond the expected timeframe, please contact our support team via email at support@bookmytest.com or call us at 01-4000000.

Payment Issues

My payment failed — what should I do?
First, check if the amount was deducted from your account. If yes, wait 24 hours for it to be reflected. If not, try booking again with a different payment method or contact your bank.
How do I confirm my payment was successful?
You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after successful payment. You can also check your booking status in the "My Bookings" section of your account.
Can I pay using eSewa or Khalti?
Yes! We accept payments through eSewa, Khalti, and major credit/debit cards. Simply select your preferred payment method during checkout.

Account

How can I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
How do I update my personal details?
Log into your account, go to "Profile Settings", and update your information. Make sure to save changes before exiting.
How can I delete my account?
To delete your account, please contact our support team at support@bookmytest.com with your account details. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
