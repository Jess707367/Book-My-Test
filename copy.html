What it is

IELTS is an international standardized test of English proficiency accepted by over 10,000 institutions worldwide.

Test Format

Four comprehensive modules designed to test your English skills:

Listening (30 min)
Reading (60 min)
Writing (60 min)
Speaking (11-14 min)

Eligibility

Anyone aged 16 or above, regardless of background, can take the IELTS exam. No prerequisites required!

Required Documents

Valid passport or national ID
Recent passport-sized photograph
Proof of payment (if required)
