What it is

IELTS is an international standardized test of English proficiency accepted by over 10,000 institutions worldwide.

Test Format

The test consists of four modules — Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking — lasting approximately 2 hours and 45 minutes.

Eligibility

Anyone aged 16 or above, regardless of background, can take the IELTS exam.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport or national ID
  • Recent passport-sized photograph
  • Proof of payment (if required)

Result Timeline

Results are typically available within 3–5 days for computer-based tests, or 13 days for paper-based.

Fees (Range)

Test fees generally range from $200 – $250, depending on location and format.

How to Book in the App

Open the “Book My Test” app → Choose IELTS → Select your preferred date & center → Complete payment.

Mini FAQ

Q: How many times can I take the test?

A: You can take IELTS as many times as you wish.

Q: Is it available online?

A: Yes, IELTS offers both paper-based and computer-based options.
