Refund & Reschedule Policy

At Book My Test, we understand that plans can change. Our Refund & Reschedule Policy ensures fairness, transparency, and flexibility for all users who book tests through our platform.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be applicable under specific conditions depending on the test provider’s terms. A refund request will be accepted only if it meets the following criteria:

  • The cancellation request is made within the specified timeframe before the test date.
  • The test center or provider approves the cancellation and refund process.
  • No part of the service has already been utilized (e.g., registration confirmation issued).

2. Refund Process

Once your refund request is approved:

  • The amount will be refunded to your original payment method (eSewa, Khalti, connectIPS, or card).
  • Processing time may take up to 7–10 business days, depending on your payment provider.
  • Service charges or gateway fees (if any) may be deducted from the refundable amount.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

The following cases are not eligible for a refund:

  • Failure to appear or attend the scheduled test without notice.
  • Late cancellations made after the permitted deadline.
  • Incomplete or invalid payment details provided during booking.
  • Test center refusal due to misconduct, document issues, or non-compliance with policies.

4. Rescheduling Policy

You may request to reschedule your test date if the test provider allows it. Rescheduling requests are subject to availability and the following conditions:

  • Requests must be made within the permitted reschedule window before the original test date.
  • Rescheduling fees (if applicable) must be paid at the time of modification.
  • Once confirmed, the new test date will replace the original booking.

5. Test Center Cancellations or Delays

In the rare case that a test is postponed or canceled by the test provider or center due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., weather, technical issues, or public emergencies), users will be notified and offered either:

  • A rescheduled date at no additional cost, or
  • A full refund, depending on the provider’s terms.
