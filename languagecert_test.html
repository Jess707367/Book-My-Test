What it is

Language Cert evaluates reading, writing, and math skills critical for success in college.

Test Format

The test is divided into three parts — Speaking & Writing, Reading, and Listening — and takes around 2 hours to complete on a computer.

Eligibility

Anyone aged 16 or above can take the Academic. Candidates under 18 require parental consent.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (mandatory)
  • Proof of payment (if applicable)
  • Booking confirmation email

Result Timeline

Results are typically available within 24–48 hours after taking the test.

Fees (Range)

The Language Cert Academic fee ranges from $185 – $250, depending on your country and test center.

How to Book in the App

Open the “Book My Test” app → Choose Language Cert → Select your test center and date → Upload your ID → Complete payment.

Mini FAQ

Q: How long are Language Cert scores valid?

A: Language Cert scores are valid for 2 years from the test date.

Q: Can I reschedule or cancel my test?

A: Yes, you can reschedule or cancel up to 14 days before your test date.
